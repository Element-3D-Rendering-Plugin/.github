## 🚀 Introduction
Element 3D for Windows is a GPU-accelerated plugin from Video Copilot that adds a real-time 3D engine to Adobe After Effects. It gives motion designers and VFX artists a way to import 3D models, extrude text and shapes, and render finished objects without leaving their compositing timeline. Because the plugin draws on your graphics card, scenes stay interactive as you move the camera and adjust materials. Across the current element 3d v2 generation, the tool has grown into a dependable workhorse for titles, product shots, and layered 3D graphics.

## ⭐ Highlights

### Modeling and Import
- **Bring in 3D models** — Import OBJ and common 3d element formats so any 3d element or prop lands straight in your composition.
- **Extrude text and shapes** — Convert After Effects text and mask paths into solid, beveled geometry with adjustable depth.
- **Reusable assets** — Combine, duplicate, and arrange objects to assemble complex scenes from simple parts.

### Rendering and Look
- **Real-time GPU engine** — Hardware-accelerated rendering keeps previews responsive even on dense, multi-object scenes.
- **Materials and lighting** — Apply reflections, textures, and environment maps to finish each element 3d model with a polished surface.
- **Native camera and lights** — Animate with After Effects cameras and lights and keyframe every setting over the timeline.
